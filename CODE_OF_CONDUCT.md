# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, technical, and direct collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Keep discussions grounded in reproducible behavior, logs, benchmarks, or code.
- Make room for contributors with different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory or degrading language.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Spam, deliberate disruption, or repeated bad-faith arguments.

## Maintainer role

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that undermine a healthy working environment.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that crosses the line, contact the repository maintainers privately through GitHub. Include links, screenshots, or any other context that helps establish what happened.

Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as discreetly as possible.
